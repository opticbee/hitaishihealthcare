Frequently Asked Questions

How long should I fast before a blood test?

Most fasting blood tests require 8-12 hours of fasting. You can drink water during this period. Avoid coffee, tea, and smoking.

When will I receive my test results?

Routine tests typically take 24-48 hours. Specialized tests may take 3-5 business days. You'll receive results via email or our patient portal.

Do I need a doctor's prescription?

No prescription needed for standard tests. However, some insurance providers may require a referral for coverage.

Can I drink water before a fasting blood test?

Yes, you can and should drink water to stay hydrated. Avoid other beverages like tea, coffee, or juice.
