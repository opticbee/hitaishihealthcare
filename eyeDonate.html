WHY PLEDGE?

THE GIFT OF SIGHT CAN CHANGE LIVES

Every year, millions suffer from corneal blindness — a condition that can be cured through a simple act of kindness: eye donation. Your pledge to donate eyes can restore vision, dignity, and independence to someone who has spent life in darkness.

When you pledge to donate your eyes, you're not just offering an organ — you're offering hope. Your decision can help two individuals regain their vision, allowing them to see their loved ones, live independently, and rejoin society with confidence.

In countries like India, where corneal blindness is widespread, the demand for donor eyes far exceeds the supply. Despite medical advancements, this shortage continues due to low awareness and misconceptions about eye donation.

Eye Donation is Simple

Eye donation is a straightforward, meaningful act that takes only a few minutes to pledge, yet its impact lasts a lifetime. There is no cost to the donor or their family, and it does not delay or interfere with traditional funeral arrangements.

Anyone can pledge to donate their eyes, regardless of age, gender, or medical history. All it takes is the decision to register and share your wish with your family, so they can fulfill your pledge at the time of passing.

With proper care, donated eyes can restore sight to two people suffering from corneal blindness. One simple act can light up someone’s world forever.

The Gift of Vision

Eye donation is one of the most precious gifts one can give. Corneal blindness affects millions worldwide, but through your generous pledge, we can restore sight to those in need.

The process is simple, doesn't interfere with funeral arrangements, and brings hope to individuals and families affected by vision loss.

By pledging to donate your eyes, you commit to giving someone the ability to see their loved ones, experience nature's beauty, and regain independence.

The Gift of Sight: Eye Donation Process

Your decision to donate eyes can bring light to two lives. The entire process is dignified, quick, and causes no delay to funeral arrangements.

1

Pledge to Donate

Register through our quick online form or at your local eye bank. Share your decision with loved ones.

2

Timely Notification

Upon passing, your family or healthcare provider contacts the eye bank immediately (within 6 hours).

3

Dignified Recovery

Trained technicians perform a 20-minute procedure that preserves appearance and allows open-casket funerals.

4

Vision Restoration

Corneas are transplanted within days, often restoring sight to recipients with corneal blindness.
