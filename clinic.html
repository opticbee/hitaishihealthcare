Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and policies.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, and UnitedHealthcare. Please contact our billing department to verify your specific plan coverage.

How do I prepare for my first appointment?

For your first visit, please arrive 15 minutes early to complete paperwork. Bring your insurance card, photo ID, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results from previous providers.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

We follow all CDC guidelines including enhanced cleaning protocols, mandatory masking, social distancing in waiting areas, and offering telehealth options when appropriate. All staff are fully vaccinated.

Do you offer telehealth services?

Yes, we offer telehealth visits for many types of appointments. These are conducted through our secure patient portal and are available for both new and existing patients when appropriate for the medical concern.
