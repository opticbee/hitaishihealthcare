INFERTILITY ASSESSMENT: Comprehensive evaluation to diagnose fertility issues in men and women.
IVF Treatment: A process where eggs are fertilized by sperm outside the body and implanted into the uterus.
ICSI Treatment: An advanced IVF technique involving the injection of a single sperm directly into an egg.
Fertility Surgeries: Includes laparoscopy or hysteroscopy to remove obstacles to conception.
Genetic Testing: Preimplantation Genetic Testing (PGT) to screen embryos for genetic disorders.
Freezing Services: Secure cryopreservation of eggs, sperm, or embryos for future use.
Oncofertility: Fertility preservation options for cancer patients before treatment.
IUI (Intrauterine Insemination): A procedure where sperm is placed directly into a woman’s uterus to enhance fertilization.
Surrogacy Program: A legal arrangement where another woman carries and delivers a child for intended parents.

IVF Centres in India +
India hosts numerous reputed IVF centres equipped with state-of-the-art technology. Top cities include Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, and Hyderabad. These centres offer high success rates and are staffed by experienced professionals. Many centres are accredited and follow international treatment protocols.
IVF Doctors in India +
IVF doctors in India are highly qualified, many with international training and years of experience in reproductive medicine. They specialize in infertility diagnostics, assisted reproductive techniques, and personalized patient care. Consultations often include pre-treatment evaluations, protocol design, and post-treatment follow-ups.
IVF Cost in India +
IVF treatment in India is more affordable compared to many other countries. The cost typically ranges between ₹1,00,000 to ₹2,50,000 per cycle. Factors affecting the cost include clinic reputation, technology used, number of cycles required, and additional procedures like ICSI, PGT, or embryo freezing.
