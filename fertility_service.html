In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF)

Evidence-based protocols for optimal success rates.

1

Ovarian Stimulation (8-14 days)

Customized medication protocols (injectable gonadotropins) to develop multiple follicles with daily monitoring via ultrasound and bloodwork.

2

Egg Retrieval (15-20 mins)

Transvaginal ultrasound-guided procedure under mild sedation to collect eggs from ovarian follicles. Typically yields 8-15 eggs per cycle.

3

Embryo Culture (3-5 days)

Fertilized eggs develop into embryos in our state-of-the-art time-lapse incubators with continuous monitoring by embryologists.

4

Embryo Transfer (5 mins)

Selected embryo(s) transferred to the uterus using a thin catheter. No anesthesia required. Pregnancy test scheduled 10-14 days later.

Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI)

Advanced technique for severe male factor infertility.

Sperm Selection

Using high-magnification microscopy (IMSI at 6000x) and hyaluronan binding to identify the healthiest sperm with optimal DNA integrity.

Microinjection

A single selected sperm is immobilized and injected directly into the egg cytoplasm using precision micromanipulation equipment.

Fertilization Check

Embryologists confirm successful fertilization within 18 hours and monitor embryo development in time-lapse incubators.

ICSI Indications

Low Sperm Count

Poor Motility

Abnormal Morphology

TESA/TESE Cases

Preimplantation Genetic Testing (PGT)

Cutting-edge NGS technology for embryo screening.

PGT-A (Aneuploidy Screening)

Comprehensive chromosome screening identifies embryos with correct chromosome numbers (euploid) to reduce miscarriage risk and increase implantation rates.

PGT-M (Monogenic Disorders)

Screens for specific genetic diseases like cystic fibrosis, sickle cell anemia, or Huntington's disease in families with known genetic risks.

PGT-SR (Structural Rearrangements)

For patients with balanced translocations or chromosomal rearrangements to identify embryos with normal or balanced chromosomal content.

PGT Benefits

1

Higher Pregnancy Rates

Euploid embryos have 60-70% implantation rate vs 30% untested

2

Lower Miscarriage Risk

Reduces miscarriage risk from 25% to <5% by avoiding aneuploid embryos

3

Single Embryo Transfer

Enables safe single embryo transfer while maintaining high success rates

4

Genetic Disease Prevention

Eliminates risk of passing on known genetic conditions to offspring
