Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are common ENT issues? +
Common issues include hearing loss, sinus infections, and throat infections. Consult an ENT for tailored advice.
When should I see an ENT specialist? +
See an ENT if you experience persistent ear, nose, or throat symptoms lasting more than two weeks.
Are hearing aids covered by insurance? +
Coverage varies by provider; check with your insurance company for specifics.

Common ENT Procedures

ENT specialists perform various procedures to treat conditions effectively:

  • Tonsillectomy for chronic tonsillitis
  • Septoplasty to correct a deviated septum
  • Cochlear implant surgery for severe hearing loss
  • Adenoidectomy for breathing issues in children

Health Tips

Maintain your ENT health with these practical tips:

  • Stay hydrated to prevent throat dryness
  • Avoid loud noises to protect your hearing
  • Use a humidifier to ease nasal congestion
  • Practice good oral hygiene to reduce throat infections

Seasonal ENT Care

With the monsoon season in full swing as of 04:35 PM IST, Friday, June 20, 2025, protect your ears, nose, and throat from seasonal challenges like allergies and infections.

  • Use antihistamines for allergy relief
  • Keep ears dry to avoid infections
  • Consult an ENT for persistent symptoms
