The MRI Experience

1

Preparation

Our team will guide you through the preparation process, which may include changing into comfortable clothing and removing metal objects. For certain exams, contrast material may be administered.

2

Positioning

You'll be comfortably positioned on the scanning table, with specialized coils placed around the area being examined. Our wide-bore design ensures a more open, less confined experience.

3

Scanning

The table will move into the magnet, and the scan will begin. You'll hear rhythmic knocking sounds as images are acquired. Our noise-reduction technology makes the experience quieter than traditional MRI systems.

4

Results

Our radiologists will analyze the images, often with AI assistance, and provide a detailed report to your physician. Many results are available within 24 hours through our secure patient portal.
